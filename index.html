Research

Conference Presented a poster titled “A covert channel attack using sound beeps in Android” in “2nd ISEA International Conference on Security & Privacy(ISEA-ISAP 2018)” held at MNIT Jaipur.
M.Tech. Thesis Thesis Title: Covert Channel Attacks and Mitigation in Android
Abstract: Android being most popular technology nowadays for smartphones, had led to enormous number of applications for Android on Google Play Store and other third party app stores. This arises confidential data leakage problem at large scale. Covert channel create possibility to leakage of information over channel which is not intended to transfer the data. In this project, covert channel attacks were demonstrated and proposed a possible solution to stop such attacks using logs monitoring.
B.Tech Project Title: Case-Based Reasoning(CBR) Implementation for Risk Analysis in Smart Grid
Abstract: Smart Grid is a network which is used to supply electricity and the changes in usage are detected through digital communication devices. In this project, CBR approach was used to perform the risk analysis of the Smart Grid network.
